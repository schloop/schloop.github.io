Our Story

Sarah and Daniel met through a chain of mutual friends in December of 2013. Since Sarah was in the US and Daniel was in Germany, their relationship grew via WhatsApp and Skype. Daniel first visited Sarah at college in September of 2014. They maintained their long-distance relationship and regularly visited each other in Germany and the US.

In August of 2018, Sarah moved to Germany and they've been living together in Frankfurt ever since!





Wedding Day

3pm — Ceremony

St. Bonifatius

Liebigstr. 28
35392 Giessen

St. Bonifatius is the church Daniel grew up in. It is a beautiful Catholic church built in the neo-Gothic style in 1902. In 2015, the church installed new, state-of-the-art organs that you will hear on our wedding day!

5pm — Reception

Heyligenstaedt

Aulweg 41
35392 Giessen

Heyligenstaedt is an industrial-style, swanky restaurant and hotel with a bright and modern event space. The property used to be a machinery factory, but has since been remodeled and is a historic monument.

Info We would love to have everyone begin our celebration with us at the church. Please be sure to arrive about 15 minutes early. After the ceremony, you will be led to the reception venue, which is a 10 minute walk down the street.

Directions

This Google Map shows how to reach St. Bonifatius church from the Giessen Main Station by foot. The path is about a 10 minute walk. You will most likely be able to see its tall, gray steeple from afar. We prefer the 750m path that passes by the Mathematikum.
